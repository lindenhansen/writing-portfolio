filament begs for the unspooling
on the corner at Flamingo Nails
stacked in threes and fives not sevens
hollowed and gleaned clink its ribs
nearby a thick reamed manual flutters
hurtling sirens to the wind

july days warm pages yellow
each thread a rebel pledge
to live freely! without thought or mind
the plastic melts a tinge
dare scaffolding unwind?
crumble like hay bales unclipped

it lies in lukewarm callous
for when icy polar plunge
come knocketh at the door
release the wire from its wrap
in a burst of splintering sap
